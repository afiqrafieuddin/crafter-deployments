Our Story

Kopi Haus started with a simple idea: create a space where people can slow down, savor excellent coffee, and connect with their community.

We source our beans from sustainable farms across Southeast Asia and roast them right here in small batches. The result? Coffee that's rich, complex, and downright delicious.

Whether you're here for your morning ritual, a midday break, or to catch up with friends—we're glad to have you.

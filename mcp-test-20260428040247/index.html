Our story

A small shop with big dreams, serving our community one cup at a time since 2015

My name is Sarah, and this shop is my love letter to great coffee. I started The Daily Grind after spending years working as a barista and realizing something was missing in most places — genuine care, without the pretension.

We roast our beans in-house on a small batch roaster that's seen more mornings than I can count. You can smell it when you walk in — that warm, toasty smell that doesn't come from an industrial plant across the country.

Every bag we sell, every drink we make, goes through the same process. My husband Mike and I taste every single batch. If it doesn't make us smile, it doesn't leave the shop.

This isn't about being fancy. It's about doing things right and treating every customer like they're coming over to our kitchen for coffee. Because honestly, they are.

A Labor of Love

We're not just another coffee shop. We're your third place—the spot between home and work where you can breathe.

Kopi Haus started with a simple idea: coffee should bring people together. In 2019, we opened our doors with a single espresso machine and a big dream. Today, we're proud to serve our community with coffee that's ethically sourced and roasted right here in small batches.

We believe in doing things the slow way. That means taking time to perfect each drink, remembering your name and your usual order, and creating a space where you can actually relax. Whether you're here for your morning ritual, a midday break, or to catch up with friends—we've got a seat with your name on it.

Stop by. Stay a while. We'll have a fresh cup ready for you.
